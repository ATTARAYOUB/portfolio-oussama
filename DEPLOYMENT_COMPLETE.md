## 💡 Tips & Tricks

### Custom Domain (Optional)

To use your own domain (e.g., oussama-kirene.com):

1. Purchase domain from registrar (Namecheap, GoDaddy, etc.)
2. In Vercel Dashboard → Project Settings → Domains
3. Add your custom domain
4. Update DNS records at your registrar
5. Vercel provides DNS instructions

### Analytics (Optional)

To track visitors:
1. Add Google Analytics to `index.html`
2. Or use Vercel Analytics (built-in)

### Email Forwarding (Optional)

To use custom email with your domain:
1. Use Zoho Mail (free with custom domain)
2. Or use your registrar's email forwarding
